WHAT IS MOLENGEEK?

MolenGeek is located in the heart of Molenbeek in Brussels. Our mission is to make the technology sector accessible to anyone who wants to ride the digital wave, regardless of background or level of training.

We are composed of three main pillars: a coworking space; long and short term training programs; and dynamic events, such as Hackathons, Geektalks, etc.
